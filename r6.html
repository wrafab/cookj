Wonderful! Skateboards!

First I thought about what I wanted on my skateboard. Then I rastered the image onto the board with the Epilog Engraver. A Partner was needed to help put the board together. I taped a bag together and pumped the air out to make sure the board dried properly. After the board dried I sanded it together so it didn't look like trash.
